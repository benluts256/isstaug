About ISSTA

We Are ISSTA Uganda

ISSTA mobilises grassroots communities across Africa to achieve sustainable development through the formation

Background

ISSTA has a mission to make contributions to social development and as such, promotes inclusive growth through facilitating small holder farmers (SHFs) to mobilize themselves into feasible grassroots socioeconomic structures, mainly Cooperatives and Village Savings and Loans Associations (VSLAs), under specific value chains, producing and marketing food and tradable products like grain, cocoa, coffee, fish, fruits, and natural ingredients for cosmetics and pharmaceuticals. This approach has proven useful for poverty eradication, Food and Nutrition Security (FNS), and transforming SHFs’ mindsets from subsistence to focused commercial activity. ISSTA also works to improve the target group’s operating environment by building civic engagement capacities, which impact service delivery, sustainable environment resource use, democratic practice, human rights, peace, and justice.

Purpose

ISSTA’s Purpose is to localise sustainable development in all its facets, through the identification of socioeconomic problems and stakeholders’ participatory design and implementation of interventions that ensure inclusive growth amongst grassroots communities in Uganda, and across Africa

ISSTA Key Objective

To catalyse social development with inclusive growth by mobilising smallholder farmers, cooperatives, and MSMEs, to engage in market-focused value chains and promote agri-business, food and nutrition security, sustainable environmental use, and civic engagement

ISSTA Specific Objectives

(i) To mobilise smallholder farmers (SHFs) in the formation of cooperatives for competitive agribusiness, with women, youth, and deprived persons’ inclusion and engaging in participatorily selected feasible value chains (ii) To mainstream food and nutrition security, and prudent environmental resources use through the promotion of sustainable and conservation agriculture among target groups activity (iii) To build community-based capacities in engaging duty bearers towards the promotion of service delivery, human rights, peace, and justice ISSTA Target Group ISSTA works with development stakeholders who include grassroots communities, funders from all countries of the world, regional development blocks, central governments, local governments, academia, faith-based organisations, International and National NGOs, and Community-Based Organisations (CBOs)

Programmes

Strategic Interventions

ISSTA targets grassroots smallholder farmers (SHFs), fisher communities, artisans, and traders, to promote cooperatives progressively mainstreamed with inclusiveness, productivity, and market competitiveness ISSTA promotes social enterprise to achieve social development and institutional sustainability The ISSTA inclusive growth concept mainstreams youth, women, and deprived persons into gainful activities ISSTA promotes civic engagement to promote duty bearers’ accountability in the delivery of services, democracy, human rights, peace, and justice

Activities, Outputs, Outcomes, and Impact Areas Increase community-level inclusive socio-economic structuring, productivity, competitiveness, and sustainability Poverty eradication and food and nutrition security Promoting civic engagement to impact service delivery in education, health, water and sanitation, environmental resource management, democratic practice, human rights, peace and justice Provides support services to CSOs and development partners in baseline studies, social research, and term evaluations

Vision

"Empowered communities driving inclusive and sustainable development across generations."

Mission

"To advance sustainable social development by empowering grassroots communities and influencing socio-economic systems at local, national, and regional levels."

Core Values

ISSTA embraces the following core principles:

  • Integrity & Accountability: We act ethically, responsibly, and are answerable for our actions and resources.
  • Transparency: We communicate openly and honestly with stakeholders.
  • Community-Centred Action: Communities are at the heart of our decisions, solutions, and partnerships.
  • Teamwork & Shared Responsibility: We value collaboration, mutual respect, and collective ownership of results.
  • Results & Impact: We focus on measurable outcomes that improve lives.
  • Equity & Fairness: We promote inclusion, justice, and equal opportunity for all.
  • Peaceful Engagement: We advance dialogue, coexistence, and non-violent approaches to development.
