About ISSTA

We're Leader In Sustainable Development

ISSTA is a social development non-profit organisation based in Uganda, established in 2022 and networks with stakeholders across Africa. It has a mission to make contributions to sustainable development and promote inclusive growth through facilitating farmers to mobilise themselves into feasible economic entities like Village Savings and Loans Associations (VSLAs), and Cooperatives for agri-business in various value chains, currently producing food crops, grain, fish, fruit, natural ingredients for cosmetics and pharmaceuticals, cocoa, and coffee. Such social development structures have proved useful for job creation, poverty eradication, food and nutrition security, export promotion, and rural economic strengthening. Within the same structures, ISSTA promotes civic engagement as a driver for improvements in service delivery, accountability, democratic practice, human rights, peace, and justice.
