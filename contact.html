ISSTA

ISSTA mobilises grassroots communities across Africa to achieve sustainable development through the formation and legitimisation of competitive agribusiness structures, such as cooperatives, ultimately connecting them to local and export markets. It also promotes civic engagement towards inclusive growth, democracy, rights, quality service delivery, and lasting peace.
